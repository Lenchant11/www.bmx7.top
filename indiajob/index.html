Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our remote work platform

Is there any fee to join? +

No, there is no fee to create a profile and get matched with opportunities. Companies pay us to find qualified professionals, so our service is free for job seekers.

How does the payment process work? +

Companies pay directly for completed work. Payment terms are agreed upon before starting any assignment, and most companies process payments weekly or upon project completion.

What kind of remote work is available? +

We have opportunities across various categories including customer support, data entry, content writing, virtual assistance, and online research. The available roles depend on current company needs.

How will companies contact me? +

After you're matched with an opportunity, the company will contact you through the platform. For some roles, initial communication may happen via Telegram for faster coordination.
